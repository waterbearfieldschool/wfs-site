We're excited to announce that registration is now open for the Winter 2026 Foundations Sequence!

## About the Sequence

This 4-week cohort program covers four essential foundation areas:

1. **Energy** - Off-grid solar power systems
2. **Air** - Air filtration and indoor air quality
3. **Communication** - Mesh radio networks
4. **Transport** - DIY bike trailers

## What You'll Learn

Each week focuses on hands-on skills:

- Building and testing solar charging systems
- Constructing DIY air purifiers
- Programming mesh radio communicators
- Fabricating bike trailer components
