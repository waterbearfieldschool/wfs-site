## Bamboo Basketry Workshop

Learn the art of bamboo basket weaving using traditional hexagonal weave techniques. In this hands-on workshop, you'll create a beautiful, functional basket while developing skills in fiber preparation, weaving patterns, and working with specialized basketry tools.

### What You'll Learn

- Identifying and preparing bamboo fibers for weaving
- Technical preparation stages for bamboo strips
- Hexagonal weaving patterns and techniques
- Using specialized basketry tools
- Understanding bamboo properties and selection
- Finishing techniques for durable, long-lasting baskets

### Workshop Details

Each participant will complete their own hexagonal weave bamboo basket to take home. The workshop covers both the traditional techniques passed down through generations and the practical skills needed to continue this craft independently.

All tools and materials are provided. This workshop is suitable for beginners and experienced crafters alike.
