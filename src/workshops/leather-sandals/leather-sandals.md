## Leather Sandal Workshop

Create your own pair of comfortable, durable leather sandals using time-tested traditional methods. This hands-on workshop guides you through the entire process from design to finishing.

### What You'll Learn
- Leather selection and preparation
- Pattern making and cutting techniques
- Traditional stitching and assembly methods
- Finishing and waterproofing techniques

### Workshop Details
Perfect for anyone interested in sustainable fashion, traditional crafts, or simply wanting a unique pair of handmade sandals. No prior experience necessary.
