## Leather Sandal Making Workshop

Create your own custom leather sandals from scratch, starting with your foot measurements and ending with a finished pair of adjustable sandals. This workshop introduces traditional leatherworking techniques including pattern making, cutting, stitching, and finishing.

### What You'll Learn

- Creating patterns from your own foot measurements
- Selecting and working with leather
- Cutting leather accurately and efficiently
- Sellier (saddle) stitching technique for durable seams
- Installing buckles and hardware
- Finishing techniques for professional results
- Designing adjustable single-strap sandal models

### Workshop Details

Each participant creates a pair of adjustable leather sandals in their chosen color. You'll learn the complete process from measurement to finished product, gaining skills that transfer to other leatherworking projects.

No prior leatherworking experience is required. All materials and tools are provided. The techniques learned can be applied to repairs and future sandal-making at home.
