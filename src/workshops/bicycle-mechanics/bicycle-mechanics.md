## Bicycle Mechanics Workshop

Master the fundamentals of bicycle maintenance and repair. Learn how to service your bike and perform common repairs to keep it running smoothly and safely.

### What You'll Learn
- Basic bicycle anatomy and components
- Essential maintenance procedures
- Common repair techniques
- Tool usage and safety practices

### Workshop Details
Perfect for cyclists of all levels who want to maintain their own bikes, save money on repairs, and develop mechanical confidence. Bring your bike if you'd like to work on it during the session.
