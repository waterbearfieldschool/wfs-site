## Welding Workshop: Build Your Butaphone

Learn the fundamentals of welding while creating a unique musical instrument - the Butaphone - crafted from a repurposed gas bottle. This introductory workshop combines practical welding skills with creative expression.

### What You'll Learn
- Basic welding safety and equipment
- Fundamental welding techniques
- Metal preparation and cutting
- Creative metalworking applications

### Workshop Details
This beginner-friendly workshop is perfect for anyone curious about metalworking. You'll leave with both new skills and a one-of-a-kind musical instrument.
