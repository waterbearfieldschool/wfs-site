## Off-grid Solar Power Basics

A hands-on introduction to building your own off-grid solar power systems. This workshop is designed for kids and adults alike - no prior experience necessary!

### What We'll Cover

- **Electricity basics** - Understanding volts, amps, and watts
- **Sizing a solar panel** - How to choose the right panel for your needs
- **Sizing a battery** - Matching battery capacity to your power requirements
- **DC & AC appliances** - Understanding the difference and when you need an inverter

### Workshop Details

This is a free, beginner-friendly workshop. Come ready to ask questions and learn together!
