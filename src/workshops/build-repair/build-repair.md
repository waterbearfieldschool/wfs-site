*Developing hands-on repair and fabrication skills for local food production.*

An ongoing workshop bringing together individuals interested in developing repair and fabrication skills relevant to small farms and small-scale food production.

### Example Skills and Projects
- Rainwater capture systems
- Basic welding techniques
- Carpentry fundamentals
- Hoop house construction
- Temperature monitoring
- Electronics repair
- Fabricating useful tools for your site

### Workshop Format
This is a collaborative meetup format where participants work together on practical projects, share knowledge, and build skills over time. Whether you're looking to fix something broken or create something new, this gathering provides the space, tools, and community support to make it happen.

### Who Should Attend
Anyone interested in hands-on fabrication and repair, especially those working on small farms, homesteads, or community food production projects.
