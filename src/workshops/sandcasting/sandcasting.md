## Sandcasting Workshop

*Image: [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sand_casting)*

Discover the traditional craft of sand casting - a process that uses specially prepared sand to create molds for pouring molten metal. Create your own custom metal objects using techniques that have been used for thousands of years.

### What You'll Learn

- Sand mold preparation and packing techniques
- Pattern making and mold release methods
- Safe metal melting and pouring practices
- Finishing techniques for your cast objects

### Workshop Details

Perfect for makers interested in metalworking, sculpture, or creating custom hardware and decorative pieces. No prior experience necessary - we'll cover all the safety fundamentals and guide you through creating your first cast piece.
