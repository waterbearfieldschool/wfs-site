## DIY Energy Resilience

*Image: [Taufik 2022](https://www.researchgate.net/publication/369082036_The_House_Project_A_Sustainable_Alternative_For_Rural_Electrification)*

A hands-on introduction to making, storing and using electrical energy at home. This workshop is designed for kids and adults alike - no prior experience necessary!

### What We'll Learn

- **Assessing your electrical needs** - How to power your devices for a specified duration without grid access
- **Battery storage options** - Affordable solutions for storing energy
- **Generation methods** - Solar panels, pedal-powered systems, and wind turbines
- **Measurement techniques** - Using multimeters to measure voltage and current

### Hands-On Demonstrations

- Small-scale solar power systems with battery storage and inverters
- Human-powered bicycle generators
- Gravity-based lighting solutions

### Additional Resources

Participants will gain access to live monitoring data from a residential solar installation and opportunities to contribute to community solar charging infrastructure projects.

### Workshop Details

This is a free, beginner-friendly workshop. Come ready to ask questions and learn together!
