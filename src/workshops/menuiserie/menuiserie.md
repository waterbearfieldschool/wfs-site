## Menuiserie Workshop: Woodworking Techniques

Learn traditional woodworking skills and techniques to create beautiful, functional small furniture pieces. This hands-on workshop covers essential joinery methods, tool usage, and design principles for crafting unique wooden items.

### What You'll Learn
- Basic woodworking tools and their proper use
- Traditional joinery techniques
- Wood selection and preparation
- Finishing techniques for durability and beauty

### Workshop Details
This workshop is designed for beginners and intermediate woodworkers looking to expand their skills and create practical pieces for their home.
