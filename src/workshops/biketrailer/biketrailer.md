## DIY Bike Trailer Workshop

Learn to build your own cargo bike trailer using inexpensive, readily-available materials. This design uses electrical conduit and recycled bike wheels to create a sturdy, functional trailer for around $35-45.

### What You'll Learn

- Pipe bending techniques using a conduit bender
- Frame assembly and wheel mounting methods
- Hitch attachment and safety considerations
- Sourcing and repurposing bicycle parts

### Workshop Details

Great for cyclists looking to haul gear, groceries, or supplies without a car. The design uses common hardware store materials and recycled 16" or 26" bike wheels. No welding required - just basic hand tools and a pipe bender.
