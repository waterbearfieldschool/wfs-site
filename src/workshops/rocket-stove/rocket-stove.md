## Rocket Stove Workshop

Construct your own high-performance, portable rocket stove designed for efficient, clean-burning cooking. Learn the principles of combustion efficiency while building a practical cooking solution.

### What You'll Learn
- Rocket stove design principles
- Combustion chamber construction
- Heat transfer optimization
- Portable stove assembly techniques

### Workshop Details
Ideal for camping enthusiasts, sustainable living advocates, or anyone interested in efficient cooking technology. Build a stove that burns cleanly and efficiently using minimal fuel.
