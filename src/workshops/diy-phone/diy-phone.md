## DIY 4G Phone Workshop

Build your own open-source 4G mobile phone from scratch. This project prioritizes mindful technology - no app stores, no infinite scrolling, just calls, texts, and the features you choose to add.

### What You'll Learn

- **Electronics assembly** - Connecting modules and understanding how they communicate
- **Arduino programming** - Writing and modifying firmware for your phone
- **Open source hardware** - Working with hackable, customizable designs
- **Intentional technology** - Building devices that respect your attention

### Components

- **SIM7600 4G Module** - Global LTE connectivity for calls and texts
- **ItsyBitsy Microcontroller** - Arduino-compatible programmable brain
- **240x240 TFT Display** - Compact visual output
- **CardKB Keyboard** - Physical typing without touchscreen dependence

### Expandability

The modular design allows you to add GPS, environmental sensors, solar charging, and more. Design your own case - from minimalist wood to creative custom designs.

### Workshop Details

Perfect for anyone interested in electronics, open-source hardware, or reclaiming control over their technology. No prior experience necessary.
