## Woodworking: Sheds and Shelters

Develop essential carpentry skills through practical building projects. Learn to design and construct various types of structures from sheds to tiny houses.

### What You'll Learn
- Fundamental carpentry and joinery techniques
- Structural design and building principles
- Shed, tiny house, and mobile trailer construction
- Cold frame and greenhouse building

### Module Details
This hands-on foundations module combines traditional woodworking skills with modern building techniques. Learn through actual construction projects that result in useful structures.
