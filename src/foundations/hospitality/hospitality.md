## Hospitality

Learn the skills and create structures that facilitate meaningful social interaction across differences. Build community through intentional hospitality practices.

### What You'll Learn
- Community building and social facilitation skills
- Conflict resolution and communication techniques
- Event planning and space design for inclusion
- Cultural competency and working across differences

### Module Details
This unique foundations module focuses on the human side of sustainable communities. Learn to create welcoming spaces and develop skills for building resilient, diverse communities.
