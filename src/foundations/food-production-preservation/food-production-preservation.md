## Food Production and Preservation

Master the art of growing and preserving food at small scales. Learn techniques that maximize productivity while extending growing seasons and preserving harvests.

### What You'll Learn
- Small-scale gardening and intensive growing methods
- Season extension techniques and cold frames
- Food preservation methods: canning, dehydrating, fermentation
- Seed saving and plant propagation

### Module Details
This foundations module focuses on practical food security skills, from soil to storage. Perfect for anyone looking to increase their food independence and reduce reliance on industrial food systems.
