## Heating/Cooling: How do we stay comfortable off-grid?

How do you stay warm when the power's out? Cool when it's 100 degrees? Understanding heat transfer and low-energy heating and cooling strategies is essential for off-grid resilience.

Our heating/cooling workshops teach practical approaches to thermal comfort without relying on the grid. Learn to calculate heat loss, size heating systems appropriately, and explore efficient technologies for small spaces.
