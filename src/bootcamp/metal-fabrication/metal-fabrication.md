## Metal Fabrication

Master the art of working with metal through traditional and modern techniques. Learn cutting, welding, blacksmithing, and CNC machining for diverse metalworking projects.

### What You'll Learn
- Metal cutting techniques and tools
- Welding fundamentals and advanced techniques
- Traditional blacksmithing and forge work
- CNC machining and computer-controlled fabrication

### Module Details
This comprehensive bootcamp module covers the full spectrum of metalworking skills, from ancient blacksmithing techniques to modern CNC fabrication. Build practical skills through hands-on projects.
