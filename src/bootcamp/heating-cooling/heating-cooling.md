## Heating and Cooling

Explore low-energy, sustainable approaches to thermal comfort. Learn to design and implement heating and cooling systems that minimize energy consumption while maximizing comfort.

### What You'll Learn
- Passive solar design principles
- Low-energy heating systems and thermal mass
- Natural cooling techniques and ventilation
- Insulation strategies and building envelope optimization

### Module Details
This bootcamp module covers both traditional and innovative approaches to thermal comfort, focusing on energy-efficient solutions that work with natural systems rather than against them.
