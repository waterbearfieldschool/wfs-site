## Water Collection, Storage, Treatment

Learn the fundamental skills for managing water resources sustainably and independently. This comprehensive module covers everything from rainwater harvesting to water purification techniques.

### What You'll Learn
- DIY water collection systems and rainwater harvesting
- Storage solutions for various scales and climates
- Water treatment and purification methods
- System maintenance and water quality testing

### Module Details
This hands-on bootcamp module provides practical knowledge for water independence, covering both emergency preparedness and long-term sustainable water management strategies.
