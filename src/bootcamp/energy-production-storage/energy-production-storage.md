## Energy Production and Storage

Learn to generate and store energy using local, DIY approaches. This module covers multiple forms of energy production and storage for resilient, independent energy systems.

### What You'll Learn
- Solar, wind, and micro-hydro electricity generation
- Battery and alternative energy storage systems
- Heat storage and thermal energy systems
- Gravitational energy storage and mechanical systems

### Module Details
This comprehensive bootcamp module teaches practical energy independence through hands-on projects. Learn to design, build, and maintain energy systems appropriate for various scales and applications.
